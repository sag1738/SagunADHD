Our Library of ADHD Resources

Explore a vast collection of resources, courses, and materials specifically curated to help individuals and families better understand ADHD and empower those living with it. We provide scientifically-backed courses, interactive guides, and much more to support your ADHD journey.

Understanding ADHD Basics

This course provides an in-depth understanding of ADHD, including the science behind it, common misconceptions, and how it affects individuals at various stages of life. You will learn about different ADHD types and the best practices for managing symptoms, whether for yourself or a loved one.

Managing time can be especially challenging for individuals with ADHD. This course offers strategies and techniques to help improve focus, meet deadlines, and stay organized. You'll also learn how to break down tasks into manageable steps and use tools to prioritize effectively.

This course is designed for parents, teachers, and caregivers who want to better understand how ADHD manifests in children and how to provide effective support. Topics include behavioral strategies, educational accommodations, and nurturing children's strengths and creativity.
